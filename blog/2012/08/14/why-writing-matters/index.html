Why Writing Matters

Aug 14th, 2012

Writing is a very primal instinct, like drawing or singing. We humans are social creatures who seek to explore new ideas and communicate our own. Writing provides with a way to do so in a way that transcends time and space.

Writing also opens an avenue for self-discovery. Most great thinkers and inventors were also writers who kept diaries all their lives. Leonardo Da Vinci is perhaps most well-known for his journals. Today, thanks to technology, our thoughts can be instantly shared easily with the world, and in a way that even transcends the medium. These words can theoretically live until the end of time since they are no longer tied to the medium itself.

This humble blog is my attempt at making sense of my world. Thanks for dropping by.
